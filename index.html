What is E-Waste ?

E-waste, short for electronic waste, refers to discarded electronic devices that have reached the end of their useful life or are no longer wanted. This includes a wide range of items such as computers, smartphones, tablets, televisions, refrigerators, and other household appliances that contain electronic components.

Electronic waste or e-waste describes discarded electrical or electronic devices. Used electronics which are destined for reuse, resale, salvage, recycling, or disposal are also considered e-waste. Informal processing of e-waste in developing countries can lead to adverse human health effects and environmental pollution.

Sources of E-Waste

Waste generated from the products used for data processing such as computers, computer devices like monitor, speakers, keyboards, printers etc.

Equipment or devices used for communication like phones, landline phones, fax etc.
